The 5 Features That Create Platform Lock-In
1️⃣ Earnings Call Preparation Intelligence
(Pre-Event)
This is the feature we discussed earlier.
Before every call, CuraLive generates an AI Earnings Call Briefing.
Example insights:
•	predicted investor questions
•	top investor concerns
•	high-risk disclosure topics
•	executive communication insights
This becomes the preparation tool for executives and IR teams.
Once teams start using this before every call, CuraLive becomes part of their standard preparation process.
________________________________________
2️⃣ Real-Time AI Earnings Call Co-Pilot
(During Event)
During the live call, CuraLive assists the moderator and IR team.
Capabilities include:
•	live transcript monitoring
•	investor sentiment tracking
•	disclosure risk alerts
•	question prioritisation
Example workflow:
Live earnings call
↓
AI monitors transcript
↓
Signals detected
↓
Insights displayed to IR team
This helps teams manage the event more effectively.
________________________________________
3️⃣ Post-Event Investor Intelligence Report
(After Event)
Immediately after the event CuraLive generates a communication intelligence report.
Example insights:
•	investor sentiment score
•	key investor concerns
•	engagement metrics
•	communication effectiveness scores
These reports are usually shared with:
•	executive leadership
•	the board
•	investor relations teams
Once boards start expecting this report every quarter, CuraLive becomes very difficult to remove.
________________________________________
4️⃣ Investor Question Intelligence Database
(Historical Memory)
CuraLive builds a historical database of investor questions.
Example insights:
Top recurring investor questions

1. revenue guidance
2. margin outlook
3. supply chain risk
Over time CuraLive becomes the institutional memory for investor communications.
Companies can track:
•	recurring investor concerns
•	question difficulty trends

Replacing CuraLive would mean losing this history.

5️⃣ Investor Communication Benchmarking
(Industry Intelligence)
CuraLive benchmarks companies against peers.
Example insights:
Average Q&A duration (industry): 19 minutes
