RECOMMENDED OPERATOR CHECKLIST

Before operating your first live event with new AI features, confirm you have completed each of the following:

Pre-Launch Training

  • Training Guide: Completed all 4 phases and passed each quiz (75% minimum)
  • AI Features Training: Completed Virtual Studio and AI Features training modules
  • Training Mode: Completed at least one full practice session for your event type. Scored 4 or more stars
  • Interconnection Workflows: Reviewed recommended activation sequences for your bundle

Pre-Event Setup

  • Shadow session: Observed an experienced operator running a live event
  • Headset and webphone: Tested your audio equipment and confirmed webphone registration at least 15 minutes before the event
  • Studio Configuration: Pre-configured Virtual Studio with correct bundle, avatar style, and overlays
  • ESG Setup: Configured ESG flags and compliance requirements if applicable

During Event

  • OCC status: Set your status to Present & Ready in the OCC before the event start time
  • Virtual Studio: Monitor avatar, overlays, and live metrics during broadcast
  • Sentiment Dashboard: Watch real-time sentiment, engagement, and compliance indicators
  • Intelligent Broadcaster: Review AI recommendations and operator alerts throughout event
  • Escalation path: You know who to contact if something goes wrong during a live call

Post-Event

  • Post-Event Report: Review AI-generated summary, sentiment analysis, and Q&A highlights
  • ROI Tracking: Monitor ROI realization in Interconnection Analytics dashboard
  • Webcast Recap: Generate and distribute video recap, podcast, and social content
  • Feedback: Provide feedback on AI feature performance and recommendations
