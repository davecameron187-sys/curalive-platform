BUG-LC4: Session Mutations Unauthenticated
Severity: Critical (Security)
Detail: All session mutations (approveQuestion, rejectQuestion, saveNotes, exportSession, handoffSession) used publicProcedure, allowing unauthenticated callers to execute operator actions.

Severity: High (Functionality)
Detail: <LiveSessionPanel onClose={...} /> was rendered without passing the actual liveSession data. The panel fell back to a hardcoded default session, meaning the live console would always show stale/incorrect data.
Fix: Now maps real session fields (id, eventName, status, startedAt, duration, attendeeCount, connectivityProvider) to the panel's contract.
