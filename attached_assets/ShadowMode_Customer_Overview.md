## A New Layer of Intelligence on Every Event You Run

When Bastion Group manages your webcast — whether it is a quarterly earnings call, an AGM, a capital markets day, or a CEO town hall — we now bring an additional layer of AI-powered intelligence to every event, at no change to how your event runs.

This document explains what that means for you, what we capture, and the value it creates.

---

## How It Works — What You Will Notice

**Nothing changes on your side.**

Your event runs exactly as it always has. Your presenters dial in or join via Zoom or Teams as normal. Your participants join with the same link. Your moderators manage questions the same way.

The only visible difference is that a participant named **"CuraLive Intelligence"** joins your meeting. This is our AI intelligence system. It does not speak, it does not interact with your meeting, and it does not affect the event in any way. It simply listens and works silently in the background.

---

## What CuraLive Captures During Your Event

### 1. Full Real-Time Transcription
CuraLive captures a complete, verbatim transcript of everything said during your event, attributed to each speaker. This transcript is available to you after the event and can serve as:
- A full record of all statements made by executives
- Source material for post-event communications and press releases
- A compliance reference document
- Input for your investor relations team

### 2. Live Investor Sentiment Scoring
As your event progresses, CuraLive scores the emotional tone of the conversation in real time. Every few minutes, the system produces a sentiment reading — a score from 0 to 100 that reflects how positively or negatively the conversation is trending at that moment.

This tells you things like:
- At which point during your earnings call sentiment peaked (often correlating with a strong guidance statement)
- Whether a particular announcement caused a sentiment drop
- How your overall investor communication landed compared to previous events

### 3. Compliance Risk Detection
CuraLive automatically scans everything said for language that may carry regulatory risk — forward-looking statements, guidance language, references to material non-public information, and similar categories.

After your event, you receive a compliance risk score and a flag report identifying any moments that may warrant review by your legal or investor relations team before the event recording is published or distributed.

This is not a legal opinion. It is an early-warning flag that gives your team the opportunity to review before any post-event communications go out.

### 4. Engagement Intelligence
CuraLive tracks how actively speakers and participants engaged throughout the event — when conversation was most active, where silences or drops in engagement occurred, and how the Q&A session compared to the main presentation in terms of audience responsiveness.

### 5. Post-Event Intelligence Report
When your event concludes, CuraLive generates a structured intelligence summary that includes:
- Overall sentiment score for the event
- Sentiment timeline — how it moved across the event
- Compliance flag summary
- Engagement highlights — most active segments
- Full transcript

This report is available to your investor relations and communications team within minutes of the event ending.

## The Value This Creates for Your Organisation

**For your Investor Relations team**  
You gain an objective, data-driven view of how your communication landed with participants — not based on share price movements hours later, but based on real-time sentiment captured during the event itself. Over time, as we run multiple events together, you build a picture of what messaging works and what creates concern.

**For your Legal and Compliance team**  
Every earnings call and investor communication carries regulatory risk. CuraLive gives you an automated first pass on compliance exposure before any post-event distribution happens. This does not replace your legal review — it makes it faster and more targeted.

**For your Communications team**  
The full transcript is available immediately after the event, saving hours of manual transcription work. Key sentiment peaks — moments where investor response was most positive — are flagged for use in post-event communications and social media.

**For your Board and Executive team**  
Over multiple events, CuraLive builds a historical intelligence record for your organisation. You can compare how this quarter's earnings call landed compared to the previous four. You can see whether your communication quality is improving. You can understand, with data, what your investors responded to.

## Your Data — Who Sees It and How It Is Protected

All intelligence captured during your event is stored exclusively in your Bastion Group account on the CuraLive platform. It is not shared with any third party, not used for any purpose other than your own reporting, and not visible to any other client.

The transcription technology operates under standard data processing terms. No audio or video is permanently retained — only the text transcript and the intelligence scores derived from it.

If you have specific data residency or confidentiality requirements, Bastion Group will provide a data processing agreement on request.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I need to do anything differently to enable this?**  
No. Bastion Group handles the setup on our side. The only thing you may notice is the "CuraLive Intelligence" participant in your meeting list. If your meeting has a waiting room, please ensure your host admits this participant when it joins.

**Does CuraLive speak or interact during the meeting?**  
No. It is entirely passive. It does not speak, it does not send messages, and it does not affect the meeting in any way.

**Can we opt out of specific events?**  
Yes. Simply let your Bastion Group account manager know and we will not activate CuraLive Intelligence for that event.

**Who receives the post-event intelligence report?**  
The report is provided to your nominated Bastion Group contact. Distribution within your organisation is at your discretion.

**Is the transcription accurate?**  
The transcription system performs at a high level of accuracy for clear audio in English. Accuracy may be lower in environments with background noise, heavy accents, or where multiple speakers talk simultaneously. The compliance flag system is designed to be sensitive rather than precise — it flags conservatively, meaning it may surface items for review that turn out not to require action.

**What if the event is confidential?**  
All event data is treated as confidential by default. CuraLive Intelligence operates under the same confidentiality obligations as all Bastion Group services.
