New Module 31 — Autonomous Live Q&A Intelligence Engine
Refer: FIG 28–30
Purpose
The system provides a fully autonomous Live Q&A Intelligence Engine that runs inside the existing Shadow Mode console, enabling real-time attendee question submission, AI-powered triage, multi-jurisdictional compliance screening, AGI-driven autonomous tool and policy generation, direct speaker/IR communication, and blockchain-certified audit trails — all without leaving the operator’s single console.
Detailed Operation

Operator launches session from Shadow Mode console → 8-character access code generated.
Attendees access via public link /qa/:code (no login, voice-to-text supported).
Questions flow into the “Live Q&A” tab with instant AGI triage, compliance risk scoring, and priority ranking.
Operator uses one-click actions: Approve, Route to Bot, Legal Review, Reject, Generate AI Draft, Send to Speaker (with auto-draft), IR Team Chat, and Broadcast.
AGI Corporate Compliance Layer performs predictive risk analysis across ZA_JSE, US_SEC, UK_FCA, EU_ESMA and auto-generates policies.
AGI Tool Generator analyses session patterns and autonomously proposes new micro-tools (Module 28 promotion pipeline).
Every action is logged with a blockchain Clean Disclosure Certificate.
Platform integration layer generates one-click share links and embed codes for Zoom/Teams/Webex/Meet.

Claims (New Independent Claims 46–55)
Claim 46
A computer-implemented method for providing an autonomous Live Q&A intelligence engine, comprising: (a) receiving questions via a separate link independent of the meeting platform; (b) autonomously triaging, clustering, deduplicating, and ranking questions; (c) applying AGI multi-jurisdictional compliance screening; (d) generating compliance-safe auto-drafts and sending directly to the speaker/IR team; (e) enabling real-time IR team chat and broadcast within the same console; (f) generating blockchain Clean Disclosure Certificates per session; (g) autonomously generating and promoting new micro-tools via the AGI Tool Generator; and (h) providing platform-embeddable share widgets and white-label codes for Zoom, Teams, Webex, Meet and any other platform.
Claim 47
The method of Claim 46, further comprising an autonomous AGI Corporate Compliance Layer that predicts selective disclosure risks in real time, generates jurisdiction-specific policies, and self-heals compliance gaps across multiple regulatory regimes.
