📌 Executive Summary: CuraLive is a live event intelligence platform that transforms how enterprises manage, monitor, and analyze real-time communications across all platforms. We sit on top of Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Webex, RTMP streams, and PSTN dial-ins to deliver real-time transcription, AI-powered sentiment analysis, intelligent Q&A management, and comprehensive post-event analytics. Our target market includes investor relations teams, corporate communications, financial services, healthcare, and government agencies.

Key Features by Module

1. Operator Console (OCC)

The command centre where operators manage live events in real-time. Features include conference overview, real-time participant monitoring, multi-select actions, search and filtering, Q&A management, transcription and translation, call quality monitoring, and audit trails.

2. Moderator Console

Enables moderators to manage Q&A and participant engagement with Q&A queue management, engagement metrics, chat moderation, sentiment monitoring, polls, and breakout room management.

3. Presenter Teleprompter

Large-text interface for speakers during events with live transcription display, approved Q&A questions, sentiment gauge, time tracking, and speaker notes.

4. Attendee Event Room

Participant-facing interface with live video and audio, chat and Q&A, polls, sentiment voting, breakout rooms, and recording playback.

5. Post-Event Report

Comprehensive analytics and insights with AI-generated summary, full transcription, sentiment analysis, Q&A recap, engagement metrics, and export options.
