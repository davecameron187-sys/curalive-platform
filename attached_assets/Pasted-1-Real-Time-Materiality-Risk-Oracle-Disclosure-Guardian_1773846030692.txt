Description

Why unique
No tool does real-time materiality scoring + auto-drafting + live operator intervention during the call.
Core technical innovation
Parallel evasion NLP + materiality classifier (trained on historical enforcement cases) + RAG over issuer filings + Ably pub/sub alert to OCC.
Suggested independent claim skeleton
A computer-implemented method for real-time protection against selective disclosure during a live investor communication event, comprising:
(a) continuous ingestion of an audio stream;
(b) simultaneous execution of an evasion NLP model and a materiality classifier trained on historical enforcement actions;
(c) generation of a draft regulatory filing using RAG when risk exceeds threshold;
(d) publishing the risk score and draft via pub/sub to an operator console with one-click approval.
