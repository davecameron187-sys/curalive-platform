# CURALIVE — BILLING ROADMAP
**Status: Future Phase — DO NOT BUILD YET**
**Last Updated: April 24 2026**

---

## CURRENT RULE — NON-NEGOTIABLE

DO NOT build billing systems now.
DO NOT implement pricing logic in code.
DO NOT introduce billing dependencies into the product yet.

Billing is a go-to-market layer, not a build-phase priority.

---

## WHEN THIS PHASE STARTS

Billing work begins ONLY when:
- Real-time system is proven
- Customer dashboard is stable
- Multiple sessions run reliably
- Product demonstrates consistent value

---

## CORE PRINCIPLE

CuraLive does not sell software access.
CuraLive sells confidence in high-stakes communication.

CuraLive charges for intelligence delivered and risk reduced — not basic software usage.

---

## BILLING MODEL — LOCKED DIRECTION

### Primary Unit
Session = one event-based intelligence delivery

NOT per signal. NOT per action. NOT per API call.

### Tier Structure

**Tier 1 — Starter (Pilot Phase)**
- Low monthly subscription
- Limited sessions
- Post-event + basic intelligence
- Goal: prove value, not maximise revenue

**Tier 2 — Professional**
- Higher monthly subscription
- High session cap (not unlimited initially)
- Real-time intelligence
- Governance + reporting

**Tier 3 — Enterprise**
- Custom pricing
- SLA + onboarding + integrations
- Full platform access

### Usage Layer (Controlled)
- Additional sessions billed at fixed rate
- Optional premium processing
- Must remain predictable and transparent

---

## EXPLICIT NON-BILLING METRICS

These are tracked but NEVER used for billing:
- Intelligence signals ❌
- Governance actions ❌
- Feed volume ❌

Used for: optimisation, analytics, product improvement only.

---

## VALUE ANCHORS — PRICING MUST TIE TO THESE

- Risk reduction
- Compliance confidence
- Time saved
- Decision quality
- Audit readiness

---

## PRE-LAUNCH REQUIREMENTS

Before billing is finalised:
- Run pilot customers
- Observe usage patterns
- Validate session-based pricing
- Confirm willingness to pay

---

## FUTURE BILLING EXPANSION (LATER PHASES)

- Personal Intelligence Profiles (per role/user)
- Predictive Intelligence premium tier
- API / integration billing

---

## RISKS TO AVOID

- Overcomplicated pricing
- Unpredictable usage billing
- Pricing based on internal metrics
- Building billing too early

---

## DEFINITION OF DONE (FOR BILLING PHASE)

Billing is ready when:
- Pricing model is simple to explain
- Aligns with real customer usage
- Supports enterprise contracts
- Can scale without redesign
