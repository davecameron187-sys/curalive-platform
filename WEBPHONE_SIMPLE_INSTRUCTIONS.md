## What Happens Next?

1. **CuraLive starts recording** - The system is now listening to your Webphone call
2. **Words appear on screen** - You'll see live transcription (what people are saying)
3. **Sentiment updates** - You'll see if people sound happy, neutral, or frustrated
4. **Everything is saved** - When you end the call, everything is stored

## Webphone ID Rules

Your Webphone ID can have:
- ✅ Letters (a-z, A-Z)
- ✅ Numbers (0-9)
- ✅ Hyphens (-)
- ✅ Underscores (_)

Examples that work:
- `graham-test-001`
- `internal_meeting`
- `demo-2025-03-17`

Examples that DON'T work:
- `graham test` (spaces not allowed)
- `@graham` (special characters not allowed)
- `ab` (too short - needs at least 3 characters)

## Troubleshooting

### "I don't see Webphone button"
- Refresh the page (press F5)
- Clear your browser cache
- Try a different browser

### "Error: Webphone ID is invalid"
- Check your ID has no spaces
- Check it has at least 3 characters
- Check it only has letters, numbers, hyphens, or underscores

### "No transcript appearing"
- Wait a few seconds - it takes time to start
- Check that audio is being captured
- Try ending and starting a new session
