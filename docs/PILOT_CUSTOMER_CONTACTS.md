# Phase 2 Pilot Customer Contacts & Outreach

## Pilot Customer Selection Criteria

The 5 pilot customers for Phase 2 Auto-Muting beta were selected based on the following criteria:

**Industry Fit**
- Highly regulated industries (finance, healthcare, pharma, legal)
- Frequent investor events, earnings calls, or board briefings
- Strong compliance requirements and risk management culture

**Company Profile**
- Enterprise-level customers ($10M+ ARR potential)
- 50+ employees with dedicated compliance/IR teams
- Existing Chorus.AI customers with positive relationship
- Willingness to provide feedback and participate in testing

**Technical Readiness**
- Already using Recall.ai or willing to integrate
- Zoom, Microsoft Teams, or Webex infrastructure
- Technical team available for integration support
- Monitoring and alerting infrastructure in place

**Engagement Level**
- Committed to weekly check-ins and feedback
- Designated operator and compliance officer
- Willingness to run 2-3 test events during beta
- Potential to become reference customer for GA launch

**Day 1: Initial Outreach**
- Send outreach email to all 5 contacts
- Log in CRM with outreach date and contact info
- Set reminder for follow-up in 2 days

**Day 3: First Follow-Up**
- If no response, send follow-up email
- Try calling primary contact
- Escalate to account manager if needed

**Day 5: Second Follow-Up**
- If still no response, escalate to VP Sales
- Try alternative contact at company
- Consider alternate pilot customer

**Day 7: Decision Point**
- If confirmed, schedule kickoff meeting
- If declined, move to alternate pilot customer
- If no response, mark as "No Response" and move on
